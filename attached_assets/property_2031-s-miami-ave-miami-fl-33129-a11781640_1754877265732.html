Description

Behind a private gate, this modern home offers high ceilings, a striking open balcony, and a thoughtfully designed main level with lavish tile and wood flooring. Upstairs, rich wood floors lead to ensuite bedrooms, including a lavish primary suite with oversized windows, a sprawling walk-in closet, dual vanities, and a spa-style tub and shower. Enjoy Jenn-Air/Thermador appliances, a gas range, Crestron automation, and Nest thermostat. The outdoor retreat features a heated pool, covered patio, gourmet summer kitchen, lush landscaping, and a gym/cabana. Plus, a 1-car garage and upstairs laundry offer added convenience. Just a 10-minute walk to all that Brickell Ave has to offer!
